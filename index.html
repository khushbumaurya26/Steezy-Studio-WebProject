ABOUT US

Our mission is to provide the best, most comprehensive and authentic dance education to the world through STEEZY Studio. Since 2014, we’ve taken all the baby steps and a few giant leaps to becoming a “real company.”

BALLET

Ballet is a refined form of dance originating in 15th-century Italian courts, known for its grace, precision, and storytelling through movement. Dancers undergo intensive training to achieve technical mastery, performing worldwide to captivate audiences with its beauty and athleticism.

SALSA

Salsa dance is a lively social dance from the Caribbean, known for energetic movements, rhythmic footwork, and vibrant Latin music. It's popular globally for its infectious energy and cultural flair.

POPPING

Popping is a rhythmic street dance style known for its quick, jerky movements and expressive flair, originating in the 1970s within funk and hip-hop culture.
